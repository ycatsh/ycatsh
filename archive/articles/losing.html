Losing

#personal

I was a very passionate person but I somehow lost it all. Now I'm left figuring out who decides the meaning of success. Why are some things more socially acceptable than the others? Everything boils down to meaninglessness. Expectation, luck, winning all seems trivial at the end of the day. When you lose your motivation and stop believing in yourself everything really starts to feel like nothing. I will work hard again but I am simply not the same person I used to be.
