Content

When people talk about being content, do they really mean it? As for me, I'm not content with anything. I constantly find fault in myself and sometimes manipulate myself into being indifferent to things that are paramount to leading a satisfying and successful life. I feel like if I was truly content; the emotional turmoil and overthinking associated with even the smallest mistakes would be gone.

From today, I have started to meditate. In my defense, there's a lot of misinformation about meditation on the internet so I always thought it was some sort of sham. But, to my surprise, the calmness I felt just on my first day was utterly insane. This will be my journey towards finding contentment in all aspects of life, whether they are materialistic or not.
